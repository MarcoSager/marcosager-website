€400,000 Saved Before It Was Lost

A German investor came to me days before signing a land purchase agreement. The contract looked clean. But the land title was a Nor Sor 3 — not a Chanote. Foreigners cannot own it under any structure. I stopped the deal. We restructured. He invested safely six months later.

Outcome Investment protected · Legal structure established
